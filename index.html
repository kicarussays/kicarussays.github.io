Junmo Kim

Ph.D. candidate, Interdisciplinary Program in Bioengineering, Seoul National University.

Hello! My name is Junmo Kim, and I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Interdisciplinary Program in Bioengineering at Seoul National University, where I am actively working with my advisor Prof. Kwangsoo Kim. Previously, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Management Engineering and Mathematics from Korea University in 2021.

I am interested in leveraging electronic health records and diverse types of medical data through artificial intelligence, to prevent disease progression, alleviate medical workforce shortage, and reduce healthcare expenditures.

During my PhD course, I have experienced various types of medical data, specifically electronic health records (EHRs), including OMOP Common Data Model (CDM) and Clinical Data Warehouse (CDW), and physiological signal data, such as electrocardiogram (ECG).

My current research topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Large Multimodal Electronic Health Records Foundation Models
  • Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership Common Data Model
  • AI in Electrocardiogram
